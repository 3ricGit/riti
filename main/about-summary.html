Executive Summary

Riti Association of Unemployed Professionals is a project of St Nicholas Rehab and industrial training center. Riti association aims to counter the rising unemployment menace by engaging unemployed professionals and youth in general with career building, Counselling, rehabilitation, internships and securing employments both locally and internationally.

"Riti Association will bring together unemployed professionals, re-grouping them into their diverse professionals to initiate varous business enterprises as a group"

Director, RITI Association
